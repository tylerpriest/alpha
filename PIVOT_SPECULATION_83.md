## THE FINAL RECOMMENDATION

After 83 pivots, here's what stands out:

### Tier 1: Build This (Clear gap, validated pain)

1. **Emerging Manager All-in-One** ($300-500/month)
   - CRM + Portfolio + LP Reporting + Knowledge Base
   - Target: Fund I-III who can't afford DealCloud
   - Layer cake: Start with one, add more

2. **Scout-as-a-Service Platform**
   - Marketplace for VC scouts
   - Revenue: 20% of success fees (£5k-50k per deal)
   - Already validated by Landscape.vc

3. **IC Memo Automation** (Outcome-based pricing)
   - "$99 per IC memo" not "$300/month"
   - 8vdx (YC) validated the problem
   - Saves days per deal

### Tier 2: Interesting But Harder

4. **GP-Led Secondaries Platform** - $68B market, no tools, complex
5. **Anti-Portfolio Tracker** - Novel, but VCs may not want to look at failures
6. **VC Institutional Memory** - Right problem, hard to prove ROI

### Tier 3: Avoid

- Anything competing directly with Hebbia, AlphaSense, PitchBook
- Generic "knowledge base" (Notion exists)
- Anything requiring massive data moats
- Freemium models (penny gap problem)
